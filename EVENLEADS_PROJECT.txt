# EvenLeads - AI Lead Generation Platform

## What is it?

EvenLeads is a lead gen tool that finds potential customers for your business by scanning Reddit. Instead of manually searching for people who need your service, we do it automatically.

## How it works

1. **Create a campaign** - Tell us what service/product you offer and who you're looking for
2. **We scan Reddit** - Our system finds posts where people are asking for exactly what you provide
3. **Get matched leads** - See posts that match your campaign criteria
4. **Auto-generate replies** - Click "generate reply" and our AI writes human-sounding responses based on the post content
5. **Send on autopilot** - Messages or comments that don't look like spam

## Key features

- **Smart matching** - Finds posts where people actually want your service
- **AI-powered replies** - Analyzes post content to generate personalized, natural responses
- **Human-like messaging** - No robotic spam, just genuine-sounding outreach
- **One-click generation** - Just hit generate and get ready-to-send messages
- **Free 7-day trial** - Test it out risk-free
